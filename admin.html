1. Go to Restaurants and add your venue.
2. Go to Menu Editor and add items. Paste a .glb URL to enable AR viewing.
3. Go to QR Codes to generate and download your table QR.
4. Guests scan the QR → see the menu → tap "View AR" to place food on their table!
