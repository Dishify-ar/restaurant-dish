📋 Quick Guide

1. Go to Restaurants → fill in name, vibe, owner password, ownership info and add.
2. Go to Menu Editor → add items. Paste a .glb URL or Google Drive link to enable AR.
3. Go to QR Codes → generate and download table QR codes.
4. Restaurant owner goes to owner.html → selects their restaurant → enters their password → sees live orders.

➕ Add Menu Item
📦 AR Model URL Options
Option 1 — Direct CDN link: Use a public .glb URL (e.g. from poly.pizza, sketchfab, your own CDN).
Option 2 — Google Drive: Upload .glb → Share → "Anyone with the link" → Copy → Paste here. It auto-converts.
⚠ Note: Google Drive .glb links often fail in model-viewer due to CORS restrictions. A public CDN or poly.pizza is strongly recommended for reliable AR.
